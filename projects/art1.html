Marcel Duchamp

Bicycle Wheel

One of Duchamp's first pieces, this readymade sculpture challenged the definitions of art at the time and helped kick off the Dada movement.

Back to Overview

This was Marcel Duchamp's first readymade sculpture. He created this before he even coined the term "readymade." This piece marks the beginning of a long line of readymade pieces, which would become Duchamp's signature style. This piece is unique not just in that it's Duchamp's first readymade sculpture, but in that it incorporates motion. Like many of his works, Bicycle Wheel was an early example of Conceptual Art. In other words, the concept of the artwork matters more than the physical manifestation of it. This was groundbreaking at the time and to this day Bicycle Wheel remains a piece of art that stands out, even among others of its class.
