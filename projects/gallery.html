ART THAT broke THE MOLD

the premise

Sometimes a work of art stands out from others of its time, or represents a change in the artist's style

Throughout history, there have been millions of artworks created. Many of those artworks fit into their time - unique but not different. Some however, stand out. These works may have kicked off an entirely new art movement, defined an era, or just marked an artist's change in style. Whichever the case may be, I've collected a few pieces of art below that stand out in some way.

My motivation for this collection was to showcase those that thought differently. I have always appreciated people who go against the grain and that appreciation extends to my view of art. Even if it doesn't work out, I respect the artist for trying something new. For this collection, I will be focusing on paintings, drawings, and sculpture, as they are most easily represented in photographs.
