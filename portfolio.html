Band Poster

One of my assignments for a class last semester was to design a poster in Adobe Photoshop. I ended up making two, but I'm particularly fond of this one. I wanted to showcase one of my favorite lyrics from the song "Be Born" by Tally Hall. I used stock images from Unsplash, and some basic text wrapping. I believe that less is more when it comes to design, and I think this serves as a prime example of that design philosophy.
